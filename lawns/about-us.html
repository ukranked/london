About Us

Welcome to Level Lawns and Landscaping, your destination for professional turf laying, lawn replacements, and landscaping. We are based in Rayleigh, South East Essex.

Our team has over two decades of experience delivering exceptional results for residential and commercial clients in Essex, London, and most areas of Kent. At Level Lawns & Landscaping, we specialise in professional turf laying and replacing worn-out lawns. We also lay patios and carry out hard landscaping services.

We provide landscaping services, turf laying and lawn replacements for residential and commercial customers in Essex. As a result, we will dedicate 100% of our time and effort to your project, ensuring the desired results and a high-quality professional finish for the customer. David and Steven own and manage Level Lawns and Landscaping as a registered limited liability partnership.Level Lawns and Landscaping uses skilled landscaping professionals. We pay a lot of attention to small details. For over 20 years, our expert team has been committed to maintaining the beauty of your property. Trust us to keep it looking stunning.

Our Turf Laying Services

We specialize in lawn replacements and turf laying services, using only the finest materials to ensure a long-lasting, beautiful lawn. Our team takes pride in laying your new lawn with precision and care, resulting in a lush and vibrant lawn you can enjoy for years

We offer comprehensive ground preparation, using specialized equipment to remove the old lawn, grass, and perennial weeds. The subsoil is levelled to create a semi-level surface before premium topsoil is added. We also provide turf laying services and lawn replacements for newly constructed houses

Quality Turf and Topsoil

We only use the highest quality turf, freshly harvested every morning. Our team can provide expert advice on the best turf grade to suit your needs and requirements, including shade and drought-tolerant turf for lawn replacements with low sunlight or moisture

To ensure that we can provide our customers with the finest turf and topsoil year-round, we work directly with some of the best turf growers and soil suppliers in the Southeast. We continuously monitor the quality of their products to ensure that we are always offering the highest quality turf and topsoil available
